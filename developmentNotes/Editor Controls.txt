Editor camera:
While holding 'Ctrl + Left Mouse' move mouse = Camera orbit
While holding 'Ctrl + Right Mouse' move mouse = Camera Zoom
While holding 'Ctrl + Middle Mouse' move mouse = Camera pan (X and Y axis)
While holding 'Ctrl + Shift + Middle Mouse' move mouse = Camera pan (X and Z axis)

Gizmos:
'Shift + Q' to hide the gizmo
'Shift + W' switch to translate gizmo
'Shift + E' switch to rotation gizmo
'Shift + R' switch to scale gizmo
Hold 'Shift' while manipulating a gizmo to enable snapping

Shortcuts:
'Ctrl + O' = Opens the 'open project' dialog
'Ctrl + N' = new scene
'Ctrl + S' = Save the current scene, if the scene wasn't opened from a file will open the 'Save as' dialog
'Ctrl + Shift + S' = Opens the 'Save as' dialog
'Alt + C' = toggles physics collider overlay
